Introduction

Online communication can be difficult, especially in an informal setting. This is because a string of characters can mean many different things without the body language, facial expressions, and voice inflections. For example, a simple word such as “ok” or “sure” can mean anything from excited to annoyed or sarcastic. There are some ways to get around this, such as utilizing emoticons and emojis, capitalization, punctuation, and text formatting such as italics; however, even emoticons fail to communicate well sometimes, and often there are emotions that cannot be expressed this way. I have created 15 surveys to poll many things, including whether certain methods of communicating tone are effective and to see what different phrases or words can mean without other methods of communicating tone.
